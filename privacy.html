Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Pressure Point Cleaning respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and protect personal information when you use this website, request a quote, book a service, call, email, text, or contact us through social media.

Who This Policy Covers

This policy applies to Pressure Point Cleaning, a pressure washing service by Hanson Lawn Care serving Grand Falls, Edmundston, Perth-Andover, Plaster Rock, Saint-Leonard, Saint-Andre, and nearby New Brunswick communities.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or request a quote, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, town or service area, service requested, preferred timing, property details, photos or descriptions you choose to send, and any other information needed to respond to your request.

When you use the website, basic technical information may be processed by our website host, browser, and security services, such as IP address, device type, browser type, pages visited, referring page, and request logs.

How We Use Information

We use personal information to respond to quote requests, schedule pressure washing appointments, confirm service details, provide customer support, follow up about jobs, keep basic business records, improve the website, protect against spam or misuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Form Submissions

Quote requests submitted through this website are processed through our Google Apps Script form endpoint so the request can be recorded for follow-up and delivered to Pressure Point Cleaning by email at info@pressurepointcleaning.ca. Please do not submit payment details, government identification numbers, or other sensitive information through the quote form.

Booking, Reviews, And External Links

The website links to our scheduling page, Google Business profile, Facebook page, Hanson Lawn Care, email, and phone links. If you use those third-party services, their own privacy policies and terms may apply. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

How Information Is Shared

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate the website, process form submissions, schedule or provide services, respond to your request, work with trusted service providers, comply with the law, or protect our business, customers, and property.

Retention

We keep quote, contact, and service information only as long as reasonably needed for customer service, scheduling, records, legal, tax, warranty, insurance, and business purposes. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete it or de-identify it.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information against loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification. No website or email system can be guaranteed completely secure, so please avoid sending sensitive information through the quote form or email.

Your Choices And Access

You may contact us to ask what personal information we have about you, request a correction, withdraw consent where applicable, or ask us to delete information we no longer need to keep. Some records may need to be retained for legal, tax, insurance, or legitimate business reasons.

Children

Our services are intended for property owners, tenants, and business contacts. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy as our services, website, tools, or legal requirements change. The updated date above will show when this page was last revised.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Pressure Point Cleaning at info@pressurepointcleaning.ca or call/text 506-406-8787.
